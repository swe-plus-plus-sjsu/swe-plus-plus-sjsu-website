Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)
Is prior experience with programming required to join the SWE++ program?
Absolutely not! This program is designed for beginners.
What materials do I need?
Just a personal laptop with internet access and a personal google account is required! Additional resources will be provided.
Will students be given homework?
Most work will be done during class time, but some fun challenges will be given to explore at home!
Can a student miss class days?
We strongly recommend students to attend class every week as attendance is taken. If a student misses class, it is their

responsibility to catch up with the material.
Do I have to pay to be in this program?
No, SWE++ is a completely free program!
